About

I’m Tomi Taskov, a friendly coder from near Sofia who loves turning ideas into handy tools and scripts. I design and build clean websites, fast and advanced scripts, and I’m moving into desktop applications next. My focus is reliability, speed, and a smooth UX—across gaming utilities, automation, and AI development and understanding.

Recent work includes a FiveM Anticheat, a YouTube downloader/sorter, and a Windows utility toolkit. Day to day, I mostly write in Python and Lua, with a healthy dose of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to keep things interesting.
